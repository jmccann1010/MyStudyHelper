# Math 1
### Comprehensive Reference

## Module 1: Identity and simple formulas

Identity Principle: X is X

Additional Principle: When you add two numbers, you get the sum.

Subtraction Principle:  When you subtract one number from another, you get the difference.

Multiplication Principle: When you multiply two numbers, you get the product.

Division Principle: When you divide one number by another, you get the quotient.
